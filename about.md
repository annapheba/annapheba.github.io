  <h2>Hi, there</h2>
  <p>I am Pheba, a kid born in the last century, learning to survive in the current. I am from Trivandrum, a coastal town in the southernmost state of India - Kerala. I care deeply about building meaningful relationships, learning with curiosity, and staying kind through every chapter. <br> <br> 
  
  Sentences that were overflowing in my brain as I was doing dishes, watering my plants or showering led me here. Here, I am documenting me, and everything makes me, and therefore it will always be a work in progress site. I was waiting for the perfect space to write, and then I realized I just needed to start somewhere, and here we are. Most of it might not make sense to you, but if it does, yaay. I am not entirely sure where I am going with this, but it is going to be me pouring my heart out with some human-generated content.</p>
